Welcome to the Office of Financial Aid

Attending college is one of the best investments a person can make. However, it is crucial to monitor this investment. Although Rowan College at Gloucester County (RCGC) provides one of the best values in education today, there are many sources of funding to make a student's education even more affordable. Rowan College takes a comprehensive approach to awarding financial aid. More than 67 percent of students who enrolled at the College last year received some form of financial aid.

Important Dates and News

Upcoming payment due dates and application deadlines:​

  • March 1, 2018 – Spring 2018 State Grant Record deadline — new applicants
  • March 6, 2018 – Spring 2018 initial financial aid disbursement (payment) date
  • April 15, 2018 – State 2018-2019 FAFSA filing deadline — renewal applicants
  • May 1, 2018 — 2018-2019 FAFSA priority filing deadline; RCGC Scholarship application deadline
  • May 2, 2018 – Summer 2018 payment due date*
  • June 30, 2018 – 2017-2018 FAFSA standard filing deadline
  • July 5, 2018 – Summer 2018 initial financial aid disbursement (payment) date​

*Disclaimer: Classes may be dropped for non-payment if students owe a balance on their term bills by the payment due dates.
